Efo Riro

Description

Ingredients

  • Bunches of green vegetables/efo (Spinach)
  • Scotch Bonnet
  • Smoked Mackerel
  • Onion
  • Palm Oil
  • Seasoning
  • Salt
  • Crayfish

Steps

  1. Pick the vegetable from the stalk and slice into chunks.Rinse vegetable clean, drain and set aside
  2. Chop 1 onion and set aside. Roughly chop the scotch bonnet.
  3. Place a pan on medium heat and add palm oil, heat for about 5 minutes but donâ€™t bleach
  4. Fry the chopped onions in the oil for about 2 minutes. Add blended pepper to the palm oil and stir. Move on to add ground crayfish and cook for another 3-4 minutes on low heat
  5. Add seasoning and pinch of salt. (check for salt at the later stage of the cooking)
  6. Whilst you are waiting for the sauce to cook, boil water enough to cover the vegetable. Once the water is boiled, pour over the vegetable and cover with a lid for 5-10 minutes. Strain and squeeze out excess water from the vegetable.
  7. Continue to cook the sauce till oil floats on the top, add half of the smoked fish, dried smelt fish and stir to combine. (You can add little water to the sauce if it is too dry but do not overdo it as efo riro is almost water free)
  8. Add the vegetable to the sauce and stir till all is well incorporated.
  9. Add the smoked mackerel and carefully combine (stir) in the vegetable. Check for salt and cook for another 4 minutes. Take it off the heat and set aside.
